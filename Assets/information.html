What are the causes, symptoms, and treatments for technology addiction?
Definition
When you spend more time on your tech devices such as phone and computer than interacting with real people. When you cannot stop using them although they have negative consequences. Technology addiction is quite broad considering there are many devices we interact with. In this website, technology addiction is narrowed down to two major types: phone and video game addiction. These two are the most common forms of technology addiction.
Causes
  • Due to small size of our phones and tablets, we take them everywhere.
  • Impulse-control issues.
  • Video games are designed to be addictive.
  • Psychological and emotional issues such as depression, loneliness, social anxiety, impulsivity, and distraction.
Symptoms
  • Trouble with tasks
  • Isolation from family and friends
  • Having fear of missing out.
  • Depression, irritability, and anxiety.
  • Fear of leaving phone at home.
  • Hearing phantom vibration.
  • Attemtps to conceal smartphone use.
  • Sleep deprivation.
